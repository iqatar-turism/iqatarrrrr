Discover Qatar

Explore the beautiful and vibrant country of Qatar with iQatar. Find the best attractions, events, and places to visit.

Popular Attractions

Museum of Islamic Art: Located on the Corniche in Doha, this museum showcases Islamic art from three continents over 1,400 years.

Katara Cultural Village: A cultural hub that features theaters, concert halls, exhibition galleries, and more.

Cultural Traditions

Falconry: Highlighting this ancient tradition with visuals and an educational snippet.

Arabian Coffee Ceremony: An interactive video showing the process and significance of making traditional Arabic coffee.

Upcoming Events

Qatar National Day: Celebrated on December 18, this day marks the unity and pride of the Qatari nation with parades, fireworks, and cultural displays.

Doha Tribeca Film Festival: Held in November, this festival showcases the best in local and international film, featuring screenings, workshops, and celebrity appearances.
